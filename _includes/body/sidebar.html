Services

Every business needs high quality software to stay in business. We offer services like no other: our mission is to give tools for companies that allow digitalization of business in a controlled, measurable and beneficial way.

Behavior Driven Development

We let your team to adapt Behavior Driven Development which ensures that specification is always up-to-dated, clients get what they expect and every line of code produces business value.

Quality Mindset

Every business needs to transform their business into digital one. If your software does not work, your business does not work. We give tools to your team to ensure that your software produces business value from day 0, during development and after release.

Instant Feedback

We help your team to create a development process with short feedback loops so that it is easy to make adjustments before small mistakes pile up into bigger problems.

Hands-on Coaching

Our way of working is hands-on. We are part of your software development team where we help and coach developers to ensure a smooth adaption of development process.

Tailored Development Process

We analyze, measure and create a tailored development process for your business. We give your team tools to improve, test software in different levels and automate quality checks of deliverables.

About Us
